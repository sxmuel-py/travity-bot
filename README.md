## 📈 Why TRAVITY? (SEO Keywords: *Crypto Trading Bot, Automated Trading Style*)

Traditional **Algorithmic Trading Bots** often fail during "breakouts." TRAVITY solves this by using a **Dual-Layer Signal Verification System**:
1.  **Quantitative Layer**: Statistical $\sigma$-spike detection ($2.0$ Standard Deviation threshold) ensures the market is mathematically overextended.
2.  **AI Intelligence Layer**: A **Random Forest ML Filter** analyzes RSI, Volatility, and Volume patterns to "gatekeep" trades, significantly reducing drawdowns by avoiding "catching falling knives."
